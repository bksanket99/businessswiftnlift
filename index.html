Can I still become a sponsor and/or partner?

Sponsoring and/or partnering up with the Swiftnlift Business Excellence Awards exposes your organisation to decision makers, business leaders and dynamic corporate partners who value innovative thinking and commitment to the business industry. We offer a wide range of sponsorship levels to maximise your company’s visibility. To learn more, get in touch with us. sponsor@swiftnliftevents.com

What if I'm not sure which category to enter?

Don’t worry. Just enter the individual, programme or workplace category that you think best suits your entry. If the judges recognise that an entry shows strength in another category, or consider another category would be more appropriate for that entry, they can to change any entry’s category at their discretion.

Are categories subject to eligibility requirements?

Yes. Each category has its own eligibility requirements as set out on the categories page.

I'd like to sponsor a particular category, can I do this?

If your chosen category does not currently have a sponsor, please contact us. We offer existing sponsors a right of first refusal to renew their sponsorship. Categories become open for new sponsor if the existing sponsors do not exercise their renewal right.

Can a workplace or individual be entered in multiple categories?

Yes, but please minimise the total number of entries for each organisation. This will help keep the judging process manageable. The number of entries submitted for an organisation should not exceed the following: 1]Individual categories: one or two entries in total across all award categories in this class 2]Programme and Excellence categories: not more than one entry in total across all award categories in this class. 3]Workplace categories: not more than one entry in total across all award categories in this class. The judges may merge entries from a single workplace if they consider that multiple substantially similar entries have been submitted.

Does it cost to enter?

No. The Swiftnlift Business Excellence Awards are free to enter.

How do I find out more information about becoming a sponsor?

You can learn more on the sponsorship page. If questions or require further information, please contact us.

Other than sponsoring an awards category, how else can my organisation support this event?

If you provide goods or services that might be helpful as part of an awards programme, awards ceremony, and gala dinner, please get in touch for more information on how you can become a value event support partner.Other event support partners have provided goods or services at no charge, at discounted rates, or have waived certain fees that would apply to more conventional commercial events.
